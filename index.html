ARLEG

ARLEG is a physics-driven combat sandbox.
You don’t trigger attacks.
You *become* the weapon.

No tutorials holding your hand.
No safe animations.
Only consequence.

Controls

  • Left Click (Hold) – Left arm
  • Right Click (Hold) – Right arm
  • Q / E – Grab
  • R – Switch leg control

Why ARLEG Exists

Games polish chaos away.
ARLEG keeps it.

Every win is messy.
Every loss is physical.

Alpha / Demo

  • Unstable mechanics
  • Unfair encounters
  • Systems still forming

If you want finished — wait.
If you want influence — don’t.

What to Expect

Progression

Coins earned through fights, missions, and challenges. Optional purchases may appear later — but mastery is earned, not bought.

Stats

Strength affects impact. Agility affects recovery. Balance affects survival. Stats change physics — not UI numbers.

Story

A broken world told through levels and bosses. No exposition dumps. The environment explains itself.
