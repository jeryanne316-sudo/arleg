WHY ARLEG

Mouse-driven combat feels physical.
Every limb is independent.
Physics > animations.
Mistakes look funny, not scripted.
You fight the controls.
Sandbox chaos first.

CONTROLS

Hold LEFT CLICK — Left Arm
Hold RIGHT CLICK — Right Arm
Q / E — Grab
R — Switch Legs

WHAT TO EXPECT

Coins are earned by winning fights, missions, challenges, and secrets.
Coins can also be purchased for faster progression or cosmetics.
Characters have agility, strength, endurance, grip power, balance.
Stats affect movement, impact force, recovery, and grappling.
Story told through levels, bosses, and environments.
Bosses represent different fighting philosophies and mechanics.
