About

ARLEG is built around direct limb control using the mouse. Every movement is physical, messy, and skill-based.
Master momentum, grabs, and chaos to win fights.

Controls

Hold Left Click – Left arm
Hold Right Click – Right arm
Mouse – Move limbs
Q – Grab right arm
E – Grab left arm
R – Stitch legs

Tips

Momentum matters
Pull back before striking.
Grab to control
Don’t spam punches.
Stitch smart
Power vs mobility.
