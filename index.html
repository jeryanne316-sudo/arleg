ARLEG

A physics-driven combat sandbox where every limb matters.

The World

ARLEG takes place in a fractured combat arena where motion is unstable and control is earned through practice. The world reacts to your input, not scripted animations. Every movement feels physical, imperfect, and alive.

Controls

Left Click — Control Left Arm
Right Click — Control Right Arm
Q / E — Grab with respective arm
R — Switch leg control

What to Expect

Coins & Progression
Coins can be earned by winning fights, completing missions, surviving challenges, and discovering hidden objectives. Coins may also be purchased for players who want quicker access to cosmetics or experimental builds.

Characters & Stats
Characters feature different attributes such as agility, strength, endurance, grip power, and balance. These stats affect movement speed, impact force, recovery time, and grappling effectiveness.

Story & Bosses
ARLEG’s story unfolds through environments, enemy behavior, and boss encounters. Each boss represents a different fighting philosophy and introduces new mechanics that reshape how the game is played.

Why ARLEG

Combat is not animated — it is simulated. Mistakes are visible. Success is earned. The game is as much about mastering control as it is about overcoming opponents.
