ARLEG
booting physics sandbox...

ARLEG is an experimental physics combat sandbox. Control limbs directly. Stability is temporary.

Controls

  • Left Click (Hold) – Left arm
  • Right Click (Hold) – Right arm
  • Q – Grab with right arm
  • E – Grab with left arm
  • R – Switch leg control

Tips

  • Momentum beats precision.
  • Grabbing terrain is often stronger than attacking.
  • Losing balance is expected.
  • Every fight deforms differently.

What to Expect

Coins & Progression

Coins are earned through victories, missions, sandbox challenges, and special encounters. Optional purchasing may exist later, but all gameplay progression is achievable through play.

Characters & Stats

Characters differ in strength, agility, balance, and endurance. Stats directly affect physics behavior — heavier bodies swing harder but fall faster.

Story, Levels & Bosses

ARLEG’s story unfolds through distorted environments, escalating physics challenges, and oppressive bosses. Each level introduces new rules that intentionally break player expectations.
