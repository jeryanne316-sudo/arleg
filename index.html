ARLEG
ARLEG is a physics combat sandbox.
You don’t press attacks.
You drag limbs.

This site behaves like the game.
Controls
  • Mouse – Move limbs
  • Left / Right Click – Arms
  • Q / E – Grab
  • R – Switch legs
Why ARLEG

No animation safety.
No clean victories.
You fight physics.

What To Expect

Coins from fights & missions.
Stats affect real motion.
Bosses have weight.
